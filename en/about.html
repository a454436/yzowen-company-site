ABOUT OWEN BIOTECHNOLOGY

About Yangzhou Owen

Yangzhou Owen Biotechnology Co., Ltd. is a manufacturer focused on hotel amenities, toiletries and guest room disposable products. We provide OEM and ODM services for hotels, distributors and hospitality supply companies.

Company Introduction

Yangzhou Owen Biotechnology Co., Ltd. focuses on hotel amenities, toiletries, shampoo, body wash, conditioner, body lotion, dental kits, slippers and guest room disposable products. We serve hotels, guesthouses, distributors, trading companies, gift suppliers and cross-border customers.

我们以工厂直供、OEM/ODM、包装定制、规范生产环境和批量采购适配能力为核心， 为客户提供从产品选择、包装方案、生产配套到交付沟通的一体化服务。

主营产品

酒店洗护用品、洗发露、沐浴露、护发素、身体乳、牙具、拖鞋、酒店客房一次性用品。

Customer Types

Hotels, guesthouses, distributors, trading companies, gift suppliers and cross-border customers.

Service Advantages

We support OEM/ODM service, custom packaging, product combination, sample confirmation and bulk order delivery.
